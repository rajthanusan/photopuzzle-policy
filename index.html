Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PhotoPuzzle: Photo Puzzle Game ("the App") handles your information. Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Information We Access

The App requires access to your device's Photo Gallery/Media Library. This permission is used exclusively to allow you to select a personal photograph to be transformed into a sliding puzzle within the App.

2. Data Collection and Transmission

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal identification information (PII).
  • Local Processing: All images selected from your gallery are processed locally on your device. We never upload your photos to any external servers or third-party services.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to monitor your activity.

3. Third-Party Services

The App is built using the Expo framework. We do not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that collect your data at this time.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no data is transmitted from the device, we are compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR regulations regarding minors.

5. Security

Since your data never leaves your device, the security of your photos is maintained by your device's operating system (Android/iOS).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
